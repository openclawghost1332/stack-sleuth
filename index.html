Stack Sleuth

Paste one or more stack traces or raw logs, spot the likeliest culprit, rank suspect hotspots, collapse noisy failures into an incident digest, compare two batches with a Regression Radar, look up prior incidents with a Casebook Radar, map rollout drift with a Timeline Radar, hand Stack Sleuth a full incident pack for one composed briefing, or rank several packs at once with Portfolio Radar.

Built for quick triage on a laptop or phone, whether you are explaining a single crash, excavating a noisy raw log dump, collapsing an incident bundle, asking what got worse after a release, checking whether today matches a prior incident, tracking a canary rollout across multiple snapshots, pasting one structured incident pack that should turn into an Incident Pack Briefing, or ranking a release-worth of packs into one Portfolio Radar queue.
